Added `functionp` support for when [DGD](https://github.com/dworkin/dgd) is compiled with the `-DCLOSURES` flag. Closures in DGD allow passing functions as values, similar to lambdas in other languages. Our `functionp` tries to emulate the MudOS `functionp` efun behavior.

Small change, but just in case you want to experiment with it, you have it available now in the [Hexagon repository](https://github.com/maldorne/hexagon).
