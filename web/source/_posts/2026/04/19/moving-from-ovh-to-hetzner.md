After several years hosted at OVH, we have migrated the _muds_ and the rest of the associated services to a new provider, [Hetzner](https://www.hetzner.com/). Nothing in terms of service or addresses changes for players and visitors — everything keeps working at the same URLs and ports — but behind the scenes we now have a bit more room to grow and better price-performance for what we need.

[![Hetzner](./moving-from-ovh-to-hetzner/hetzner-logo.svg){width="200px"}](https://www.hetzner.com/)


As longtime readers of this blog might remember, this is far from the first time we move the servers around. Over the years we have gone through small _hostings_, dedicated hardware at home, [Amazon Web Services](/2013/07/26/nuevo-hosting-ahora-alojados-en-amazon-web-services/), OVH, and now Hetzner. Every migration has been an excuse to clean things up, update the stack and write a short note like this one.

If you notice anything odd please let us know.

### Hosting history

A small timeline of where the servers have lived during the lifetime of the project, based in some notes I had in an old notebook:

- **¿2003?** - Hosted in two different home servers for a lont time, just testing as a hobby. Allowing conecctions from the outside was a bit of a hassle, but it was fun to learn how to do it and to have the servers at home.
- **2013-07** — Amazon Web Services (EC2) - the first time we went to the cloud, which was a big step forward in terms of reliability and performance.
- **2015-01** — OVH
- **2016-07** — Amazon Web Services (EC2) (again)
- **2017-08** — OVH (again)
- **2026-04** — Hetzner (after a long time in OVH)
